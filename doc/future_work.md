# Future Work

Several extensions can strengthen fusion discovery and validation:

## Wet-Lab Validation
RT-PCR + Sanger sequencing across top-ranked junctions.

## DNA-Level Confirmation
Structural variant assays to determine genomic origin.

## Multi-Caller Comparison
Cross-check with another long-read fusion caller (e.g., LongGF).

## Direct RNA Sequencing
Reduce reverse-transcription artefacts and improve isoform resolution.

## Expanded Replicate Analysis
Additional biological replicates to refine recurrence and concordance.

These steps will help establish fusion candidates as potential biomarkers in mesothelioma.
