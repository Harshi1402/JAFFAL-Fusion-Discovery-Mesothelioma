# Limitations

Several constraints affect fusion discovery in this dataset:

## Single Caller
Only JAFFAL was used, which may introduce tool-specific bias.

## No Matched Normal
Without normal tissue, genomic vs RNA-mediated mechanisms cannot be distinguished.

## No DNA Structural Variant Confirmation
Breakpoints cannot be validated at the genomic level.

## ONT Error Profile
Nanopore sequencing may miss low-abundance or non-canonical junctions.

## Limited Wet-Lab Validation
Only IGV visualisation was performed within the project window.

These limitations motivate future validation and multi-tool comparison.
